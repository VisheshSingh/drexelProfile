Company Profile


SoleMates is a popular local shoes retail store which has been running successfully for over 10 years now in Philadelphia, New Jersey and New York. It initially kicked off its services by undergoing trading consensus with major shoe brands in the market. The commitment of store and high quality shoes makes them a reliable store owner not just in tristate, but in nearby states as well. And there are loyal customers who come to the store from different cities and purchase shoes. Sole Mates sells all kinds of shoes ranging from casual shoes to formal shoes; from snow boots to sportswear, fancy wear and many more. All sorts of shoes and accessories are available for men, women and kids as well.

Business stratergy

For the past decade, Sole Mates has evolved as a company migrating from its traditional sales techniques to modern sales handling. The entire process is automated, thereby, making use ofmodern techniques like online records, hiring and training new skilled people, and adopting necessary changes has proven to be beneficial. In addition to this, we are planning to expand SoleMates. Having a couple of similar stores or branches will not just improve the base of the business but can satisfy the old and new customers as well. Therefore, anticipating a mammoth customer base we plan in growing our product catalog at the same time we conceive to spread out its processes to the next level and start pushing the services online to reach substantial huge audience.
